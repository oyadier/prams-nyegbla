Prampram SHS Language Department: Shaping Linguistic Excellence.



At the heart of Prampram Senior High School (Pramshigh) lies a dynamic and vibrant Language Department, a pillar of academic excellence dedicated to shaping students into being confident communicators and critical thinkers. It is Headed by the esteemed Mr. Richard Dziwornu Dzineku, the department is a powerhouse of knowledge, fostering linguistic prowess through its well-structured units: the English Language Unit, the French Language Unit, the Literature-in-English Unit, and the Dangme Language Unit.

A Multifaceted Department

The Language Department boasts a of team of twelve dedicated educators who work tirelessly to ensure students master various languages with competence. Each unit is guided by an experienced head who brings his/her expertise and passion to the subject:

  1. The English Language Unit – Led by Mad. Lydia Mawuena Matey, this unit oversees the teaching of Core English Language, which includes Oral English, Grammar, Writing, Reading and Reading Comprehension. The goal is to equip students with impeccable communication skills, both in spoken and written form, and prepare students to pass the WASSCE.
  2. The French Language Unit – Under the leadership of Madam. Ama Awenu-Prah, this unit opens doors for students to explore the beauty of the French language, broadening their linguistic horizons and fostering bilingual capabilities.
  3. The Literature-in-English Unit – Headed by Mad. Dinnah Essilfie, this unit immerses students in the world of literary analysis, nurturing their ability to interpret, critique, and appreciate diverse literary works.
  4. The Dangme Unit – Led by Rev. Samuel Agu, this unit preserves and promotes the Dangme language, reinforcing cultural identity and heritage through in-depth linguistic studies.

Nurturing Talents Beyond the Classroom

Beyond academic instruction, the Language Department actively grooms students for intellectual engagement through the Writers' and Debaters Club. This initiative provides a platform for students to sharpen their oratory and writing skills.
Students debators undergo rigorous training to participate in competitive debates, refining their public speaking and persuasive abilities. Meanwhile, budding writers contribute significantly to the school's literary scene, reporting on major events, crafting compelling stories, and producing original narratives that capture the essence of student life at Pramshigh.

A Vision for Excellence

The Language Department is steadfast in its commitment to achieving linguistic excellence, not just in the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) but in a holistic manner. It seeks to ensure that every student graduating from Pramshigh possesses the linguistic dexterity to thrive in diverse professional and social settings.
Through continuous innovation and dedication, the department remains a beacon of learning, transforming students into eloquent speakers, prolific writers, and analytical thinkers who will make a lasting impact in the world.
The Language Department of Pramshigh is indeed more than just a subject area—it is a gateway to limitless opportunities, cultural appreciation, and intellectual empowerment.
