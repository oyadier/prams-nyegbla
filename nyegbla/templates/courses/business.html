Business Course Overview



Business is a dynamic subject that teaches the principles of managing, operating, and making strategic decisions for companies. It covers a wide range of topics, including Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, Entrepreneurship, and Economics. The subject introduces students to the various functions of business organizations and the way they interact in the marketplace. Key areas of study include how businesses create value, manage resources, and respond to changing economic conditions.

In Marketing:, students learn about consumer behavior, branding, advertising, and market research.

Finance: covers budgeting, accounting, investment strategies, and financial management.

Human Resources: involves understanding employee management, recruitment, and creating effective company cultures.

Entrepreneurship teaches you the basics of starting your own business, including how to pitch ideas, manage risks, and innovate.
