Overview of School

Background

Prampram Senior High School was founded in the year 2005 as a private school. The school started with a population of 54 students but now has a population of 1207 students. The school has 529 boys and 678 girls.

The school is located at Prampram in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana, situated on a 20-acre land. She has a well-furnished administration block, a library, a science laboratory, a computer laboratory, a Home Economics block, a Visual Arts block, a dining hall, a sick bay, a sports field, and a staff bungalow.
